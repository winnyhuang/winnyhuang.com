    game flow

  • 1. Invitee creates the guest list
  • 2. Each member of the guest list is alerted and asked what they feel like eating
  • 3. Application cross-references the preferences of the group
  • 4. ShroomKing comes up with five listings
  • 5. Members vote place with their five mushrooms
  • 6. Application picks the winning eatery
  • 7. Person who bet the most mushrooms on the winning location is crowned the ShroomKing
