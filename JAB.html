Design decisions

Accumulated Debt

Amounts are summed on a per friend basis.

Swipe to Resolve
Swipe + Hold to Delete

Swiping the transaction says that you resolved the debt. Holding at the end of the swipe deletes the transaction from the records.

Swipe down
for new bill

User swipes down from the home screen to bring up the transaction form.

Tap + Hold to Round

After distributing the costs amongst the group, user can tap + hold to round costs to nearest dollar amount. The thinking was, since no really likes loose change, this small feature would be nice to have.

Simple Form

To simplify the bill creating process, we narrowed down to these straightforward fields.
