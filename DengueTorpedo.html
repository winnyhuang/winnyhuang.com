    Problem

  • Dengue disease is a pervasive problem in South America, especially in urban regions where mosquitos can easily transmit the disease from person to person.

    Solution

  • Removing standing water is one of the most effective ways to prevent dengue disease. By leveraging basic game mechanics, we can crowd-source the report and eradication of standing water to the citizens closest to the problem.

Design Decisions

Game

Users get points when they report and eliminate standing water.

Web

The web component of the application is where users can see the community leaderboard, communicate with other players, and share dengue-prevention ideas.

community focus

We designed the game and website to emphasize community achievements. For instance, the home page is dedicated to the neighborhood leaderboard.

Sharing Ideas

Users can share and respond to dengue prevention ideas

mapping of reports

By having a dynamic map of reports, users and city officials can anticipate a potential outbreak.

Sponsors

DengueTorpedo is part of the UCSF Global Initiative. In 2012, DengueTorpedo received the UBS Optimus Foundation grant.
