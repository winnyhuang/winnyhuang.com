    Fan for a Day

  • iOS app that aggregates tweets about a baseball game

    Client

  • John Seggman

    DATE

  • 2011

    TECHNOLOGIES

  • Photoshop
  • Wireframes

problem

During baseball season, games are constantly going on. Fans want to know at any moment if there's a game they must watch right now.

solution

When a lot of people are tweeting about a game, the app will let other users know the game is getting "hot." Fans can also rate games, see scores, and tweet about games through the app.
